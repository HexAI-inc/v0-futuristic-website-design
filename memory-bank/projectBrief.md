## Project Summary

NBSAP Website - A comprehensive Next.js application for managing and displaying National Biodiversity Strategy and Action Plan (NBSAP) content including parks, ICCAs (Indigenous Community Conserved Areas), biosphere reserves, and a complete media library with upload and management capabilities.
